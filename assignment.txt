The goal of this exercise is to formulate a solution demonstrating how to implement a ChatBot to answer questions about the information on a menu provided.
A running solution (online notebook, i.e: Google Colab) is desirable but not required. Conversational capability is optional.
The deliverable must have explanations about the issues faced and solutions taken.

The file "data/dataset.json" is a demo output of a Menu handled at the company. 
It contains Discounts, Service Charges, Taxes, Categories and the inner menu Items with their details (price, portions, nutritional info, etc).

Some example questions:
- What is the price of a small NUTTY BOWL?
- How many calories does the GO GREEN smoothie have?
- Which salads do you have?
- What discounts are available today?
- Which discounts include coupons?
- What items trigger a BOGO Any Smoothie discount?
- Is the price for Smoothie - ACAI ELIXIR the same in all channels?
